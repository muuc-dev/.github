# MUUC Dev

Development projects supporting the mission, operations, and community life of the church.

## Focus Areas

### Website & Digital Presence

Tools and infrastructure for maintaining and improving the church website and member-facing resources.

Current work includes:

* WordPress theme and plugin development
* Member resource systems
* Event and media management
* Accessibility and usability improvements

### Worship Technology

Projects focused on improving the sanctuary experience without requiring dedicated technical staff during services.

Areas of interest:

* Simplified A/V workflows
* Automated livestream support
* Audio consistency and intelligibility
* Volunteer-friendly controls and monitoring
* Low-maintenance service infrastructure

### Breeze Extensions & Integrations

Custom tools and integrations built around the Breeze church management platform and API.

Potential projects include:

* Data synchronization
* Reporting and dashboards
* Attendance and engagement workflows
* Member self-service tools
* Administrative automation

---

## Philosophy

Technology should reduce friction, support community, and remain maintainable by ordinary humans.

The goal is not complexity for its own sake. The goal is resilient systems that continue working even when:

* the volunteer tech lead is absent,
* documentation is incomplete,
* or someone updates WordPress five minutes before coffee hour.
